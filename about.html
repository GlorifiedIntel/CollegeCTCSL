About Us

College Staff CTCSL: Empowering Our College Family

The College of Education, Akwanga Staff Cooperative and Thrift & Credit Society is more than just a financial institution; it's a community. We are a pillar of support for our college staff, fostering financial stability and empowering them to achieve their goals. We look forward to continuing to serve our members for generations to come.

Our Roots: A Legacy of Support
