Cooperative Loans

Traditional banks can be strict with loan applications, requiring high credit scores and collateral. This can shut out many people who need financial help. Cooperative loans offer an alternative.

Loans

Our cooperative have open membership, and unlike banks, we are more interested in seeing to the financial wellness of our members rather than turning a profit. Control of the cooperative takes a democratic form with each member getting one vote. The individual financial standing is not relevant, and we do not hold different layers of control based on the ownership of shares.

The members of this cooperative, while being owners, are also customers. The size of this cooperative is based on the number of members who participate. As more members join, the cooperative has more resources to offer financial products, reduced fees, lower interest rates on loans, and higher yields on savings.

The Cooperative have the following type of loan:

  • Regular Loan
  • Emergency Loan
  • Car Loan

Regular Loan

As a member of the cooperative, you are entitled to twice of your savings as loan after six months. Interest rates are much lower than what is obtainable in commercial or micro finance banks.

Cooperative regular loans are a type of loan we offer to our members. They are typically more affordable and member-friendly alternative to traditional bank loans.

  • Eligibility: Regular loans are usually available to members who have been saving with the cooperative for a specific period, sometimes around a year.
  • Interest Rates: Compared to traditional lenders, cooperatives generally offer lower interest rates on regular loans.
  • Loan Amounts: The maximum loan amount you can qualify for is often based on your savings history with the cooperative.
  • Repayment Terms: Regular loans typically have repayment terms ranging from a few months to several years.

Benefits

Here are some benefits of considering a cooperative regular loan:

  • Potentially Lower Rates: As a member, we are more concern about giving benefits to our members and so we offer lower interest rates than banks.
  • Focus on Building Savings: We encourage our members to contribute regularly this can help you build a healthy savings habit.

Emergency Loan

This is a loan given to members for personal use to meet financial obligations. The unique feature of this loan product is the flexibility of borrowing up to 200% of own compulsory savings with the society and the loan is usually guaranteed by the member’s savings and eligible guarantors’ savings. This is an emergency loan to cater for exigencies. The tenure for this loan type is 3 months and the maximum loan amount is 30% of the member’s compulsory savings balance. Repayment is a bullet-repayment with a flat rate of 15% per month.

Car Loan

Cooperative car loans are auto loans offered by the cooperative to members. They can be an attractive option for several reasons.

  • Potentially lower interest rates: Cooperatives are non-profit organizations, so they may offer lower interest rates on car loans compared to traditional banks.

  • Membership benefits: On top of the loan, you might be eligible for other member benefits like discounts on insurance or roadside assistance.

  • Easier qualification: Credit unions often have a more relaxed qualification process compared to traditional lenders.
