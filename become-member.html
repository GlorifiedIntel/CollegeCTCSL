Membership

Being a member of a cooperative comes with both benefits and responsibilities. The Cooperative is owned and democratically controlled by our members. This means you will typically have a say in major decisions through voting and the opportunity to run for board positions. The Profits are distributed to members based on their participation, rather than just invested capital. This could be through dividends on purchases or sales. We offer our members access to things like lower-cost products. Being a member helps you foster a strong sense of community with other members who share common goals and interests.

Responsibilities of Membership

  • Financial investment: Your monthly contributions serves as your savings for the raining day. We encourage members to improve on their savings.
  • Participation: Members are expected to participate in the cooperative's governance by voting on decisions and attending meetings.
  • Shared responsibility: Every member has the responsibility to make sure that the copperative succeed in achieving its mission.
