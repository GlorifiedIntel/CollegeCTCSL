Welcome Fellow Members and Guests!

On behalf of the Board of Directors and staff of College Staff Cooperative Thrift and Credit Society, I'd like to extend a warm welcome to our official Website – existing members, new members, and esteemed guests.

It's a pleasure to see you have interest in what we do, or you desire to find out more about our Activities. Whether you're a seasoned member or just trying to find out more about what we do, your presence here on this website signifies the power of cooperation and the collective spirit that drives our success.

This cooperative society was founded on the principle of shared ownership, economic empowerment, and shared culural affiliation. Through collaboration and shared resources, we've strived to create a community where members can thrive.

If you are here to make the decision to Join us, I invite you to waste no more time, and if you are already a member, let's continue to harness the collective strength of our membership. Let's engage in open discussions, share ideas, and work together to build an even stronger cooperative society for the benefit of all.

You are welcome to our official website. Here, you will find all the information you need. I look forward to a productive and inspiring yeare ahead!

Isaiah O. Amedu

About Us

We Help Our Members To Grow Their Business

College Staff Cooperative Thrift and Credit Society is people-centred enterprise jointly owned and democratically controlled by and for our members to realise their common economic, social and cultural needs and aspirations. As an enterprise based on values and principles, we put fairness and equality first allowing our members to create sustainable enterprise that generate long-term prosperity. Managed by hard working staff, College workers, we operate according to the 'one member, one vote' rule.

To be a leading Co-operative society operating in line with best global practices; and providing the best services, without compromising integrity and standard

1. To promote the social and economic interest of members especially.

2. To promote Co-operative spirit among members, work for the improvement of their living standards and encourage the development of the Co-operative investments

3. To encourage thrift, mutual help and self-help among members and provide means whereby savings may be made

4. To grant loans at reasonable interest rates to members for productive purposes

5. To facilitate the purchase and distributions of consumer goods to members

6. To raise capital and do such other things that are reasonable and necessary for the attainment of these objectives-subject to congress approval

7. To perform other functions that are beneficial and of assistance to members and/ or their families

Why Join Us!

Few Reasons Why People Join Us!

People choose to join our cooperative society for a variety of reasons, but some of the biggest benefits include:

Shared Ownership & Democratic Control: As a member-owner, you'll have a say in how the society is run. Decisions are made democratically, ensuring everyone's voice is heard.

Economic Benefits: We work together to provide better deals and services for our members. This can include things like lower prices on goods and services, profit sharing, or access to capital.
